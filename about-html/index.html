E X C E L L O
Excello sits on a charming block in a landmarked 1850s Greek-revival style building. The lovingly-appointed space features rich mahogany woodwork, beautiful wrought-iron windows, vintage tableware hand-selected by the team, dramatic florals by Fox Fodder Farm, and a series of still life paintings the duo commissioned from Georgian artist Vitali Dvali. A shaded sidewalk patio adds to the experience when the warm weather returns.

We hope to welcome you soon.
